Description

Bring a hand-crafted, artistic touch to your 2D game with this Hand-Drawn(styles)Line Art Character Animations Pack! Each animation is meticulously crafted in a unique line art style, giving your project a distinct, charming aesthetic. Whether your game features action-packed scenes or quiet moments of character interaction, this pack has you covered with smooth animations for every scenario.

The pack includes essential animations such as Idle, Walk, Run, Jump, and Die, designed in two versions: a traditional, hand-drawn 12 FPS and a fluid, high-performance 30 FPS. You can easily switch between the two in the included demo scene to fit the tone and pace of your game. Best of all, this pack is fully customizable and compatible with any 2D Unity project.the Dimestions for each image are 1024X1024 each frame..it has Seprate Sprites for each frame Organized in folders.Each frame has Transparent Background Dive into the world of hand-drawn animation and bring your characters to life!

Note: The Asset is under deveplement and new sprites might get added!!]

(These Assets were not Drawn by hand and we rendered out thought a 3D software but no AI was used tin the creation of the Assets)


Technical details

This asset contains the following features:

  • Animations Included:Idle
    Walk
    Run
    Jump
    Die
  • FPS Versions:12 FPS: ~12 frames per animation, offering a classic hand-drawn aesthetic.
    30 FPS: ~35 frames per animation, designed for smoother, more fluid motion.
  • Unity Compatibility:Works with Unity's built-in Animator Controller.
    Pre-configured demo scene with both FPS versions ready for testing.
    Easily switch between 12 FPS and 30 FPS animations by changing clips in the Animator.
  • Customization:The animations are provided in a fully customizable format, allowing developers to tweak or modify them according to their needs.
  • Package Includes:Demo scene for previewing both FPS settings.
    PNG sprite sheets for each animation at both frame rates.

This asset is compatible with all Unity versions that support 2D projects and requires no additional plugins. It's designed to integrate smoothly into any Unity-based game, whether youre working on a platformer, RPG, or casual game.
